Tic Tac Toe

Select number of player and hit 'Start'.
First Player starts as X and Second Player/Computer is O.

How many players:

1 Player 2 Player




Keyboard instructions

If you would like to use the keyboard:

First Select # of players (defaults to 1):

'a' = 1 player; 'e' = 2 Player

Then hit 's' to Start the game.

Then hit 'r' to Reset the game.

To play the board

Set to work best with a number pad.

'u' or 7 'i' or 8 'o' or 9
'j' or 4 'k' or 5 'l' or 6
'n' or 1 'm' or 2 ',' or 3
